What We Offer

Lake Tahoe Landscaping Services

From landscape design and installation to ongoing maintenance, irrigation, decks, and seasonal cleanups — Z's Lawn & Landscape handles everything your South Lake Tahoe property needs.

Service 01

Landscape Design & Installation

We design and install landscapes that belong in the Lake Tahoe Basin. Every plan starts with the site — the granite outcroppings, existing trees, slope, sun exposure, and snow drainage — and is built around plants that thrive here: Jeffrey pine, quaking aspen, manzanita, ceanothus, serviceberry, snowberry, mountain mahogany, and Tahoe-appropriate wildflowers like lupine, penstemon, yarrow, columbine, and blanketflower.

We use granite boulders, natural stone paths, timber borders, and native mulch to anchor the design. No artificial elements, no suburban sod-and-sprinkler setups — just landscapes that fit the mountain environment and age beautifully.

  • ✓ Site assessment and custom design plan
  • ✓ Native and adapted plant selection
  • ✓ Granite boulder and natural stone integration
  • ✓ Stone pathways, steps, and border work
  • ✓ Fire-safe planting zones
  • ✓ Full installation from start to finish

Service 02

Landscape Maintenance

Consistent, professional maintenance is what keeps a Tahoe property looking sharp from the snowmelt of spring through the first frost of fall. Z's Lawn & Landscape provides scheduled maintenance visits tailored to your property's size, plantings, and needs.

We understand how mountain landscapes grow differently than lowland properties — the shorter growing season, the pine needle accumulation, the specific pruning windows for Sierra Nevada species.

  • ✓ Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedules
  • ✓ Mowing, edging, and trimming
  • ✓ Shrub and tree pruning
  • ✓ Weed control and bed maintenance
  • ✓ Pine needle management
  • ✓ Fertilization and plant health monitoring

Service 03

Irrigation & Water-Smart Landscaping

Water is precious in the Sierra Nevada watershed. Z's Lawn & Landscape designs and installs efficient irrigation systems that keep your plants healthy without wasting water — and we pair every system with plant selections that are adapted to Tahoe's seasonal rainfall and dry summers.

From drip irrigation for native plantings to smart controller upgrades on existing systems, we help you use water wisely while keeping your landscape thriving.

  • ✓ Drip irrigation design and installation
  • ✓ Smart controller installation and programming
  • ✓ System repair and tune-ups
  • ✓ Winterization and spring start-up
  • ✓ Water-efficient native plant planning

Service 04

Decks & Fences

An outdoor deck or fence at a Tahoe property has to do more than look good — it has to stand up to heavy snowfall, freeze-thaw cycles, and the UV exposure of a high-altitude mountain environment. Z's Lawn & Landscape builds outdoor structures designed for the Sierra Nevada.

We use quality materials suited to mountain conditions and apply finishes that extend the life of your investment, so you can enjoy your outdoor space season after season without worry.

Service 05

Seasonal Cleanups

Tahoe properties need specific attention at the seasonal transitions. Our spring cleanup removes winter debris, trims back damage from snow and ice, refreshes beds, and prepares your irrigation for the season. Our fall cleanup removes pine needles and leaves, cuts back perennials, and gets your property ready for winter.

Pine needle cleanup is particularly important in the Tahoe Basin — for fire safety, property appearance, and protecting your plantings from smothering accumulation.

  • ✓ Spring cleanup and bed prep
  • ✓ Fall leaf and pine needle removal
  • ✓ Pre-winter property prep
  • ✓ Post-winter debris clearing
  • ✓ Irrigation winterization and start-up
  • ✓ Fire-safe clearance zone maintenance
