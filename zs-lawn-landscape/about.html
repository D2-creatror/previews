Who We Are

Built on the South Shore

Z's Lawn & Landscape was founded with one goal: to provide South Lake Tahoe homeowners and property managers with reliable, professional landscaping done right the first time. Over more than 29 years, that commitment hasn't changed.

We're a local, owner-operated company. That means you're working directly with people who live here, who know the Tahoe Basin intimately, and who take genuine pride in every property we maintain.

We understand the Lake Tahoe landscape in a way that no out-of-area company can — the Sierra Nevada's unique plant palette, the high-altitude growing season, the fire-safe landscaping requirements set by the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency, and the special character of mountain properties in this basin.

Why Local Expertise Matters

We Know the Tahoe Basin

🌲

Sierra Nevada Plant Knowledge

We know which plants thrive at 6,200 feet — Jeffrey pine, quaking aspen, incense cedar, manzanita, ceanothus, lupine, penstemon, yarrow — and which ones won't survive a Tahoe winter.

❄️

Mountain Climate Experience

We design and build for Tahoe's heavy snowfall, freeze-thaw soil cycles, and short growing season. Every structure we build and every plant we install is chosen with the mountain climate in mind.

🔥

Fire-Safe Landscaping

We understand TRPA guidelines and fire-safe landscaping requirements in the Tahoe Basin, and we help clients create defensible space without sacrificing beauty.

💧

Watershed Stewardship

Lake Tahoe's clarity is worth protecting. We use water-efficient irrigation, native plants, and responsible site management to minimize runoff and protect the Basin's ecosystem.
