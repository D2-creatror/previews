About Z's Lawn & Landscape

29+ Years Rooted
in Lake Tahoe

Z's Lawn & Landscape has been South Lake Tahoe's trusted landscaping partner for over two decades. We know the Tahoe Basin the way a local knows it — the Jeffrey pines, the granite soils, the snowloads, and the way a well-designed mountain yard can feel like home.

Licensed and bonded in both California and Nevada, we serve the entire South Shore — residential properties, vacation cabins, and commercial spaces alike.
