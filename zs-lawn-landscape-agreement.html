Terms & Conditions
  • Deposit 50% of the total project fee ($375.00) is due upon signing this agreement. The preview has already been delivered; deposit confirms the engagement.
  • Final Payment The remaining balance ($375.00) is due upon client approval of the completed website and before the site is deployed to a live domain.
  • Ownership Client owns the final approved website content and design after full payment is received. Sac Valley Web retains the right to display the project in its portfolio.
  • Revisions 2 rounds of revisions are included. A revision round is a grouped list of changes submitted at one time. Additional revisions are billed at $25–$150 depending on scope, or quoted individually for major changes.
  • Turnaround Final deployment timeline depends on receipt of client approval and final payment. Client is responsible for providing any domain credentials, additional content, or materials required for go-live.
  • Photography Site includes 8 Unsplash-licensed commercial stock photos. Client may provide or request substitution of any images at no additional charge within the included revision rounds.

  • Monthly Care Optional monthly website care plans start at $49/month (Essential) and may be added at any time. Plans may be canceled with 30 days' written notice. Unused edit time does not roll over.
  • Cancellation If client cancels after work has begun, the deposit is non-refundable. Any work completed beyond the deposit value will be invoiced at the standard hourly rate.
